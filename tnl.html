Goals

  • to understand the neuroplastic processes of various neuropsychiatric syndromes in order to enable targeted therapy

Task

  • to complement the range of treatments offered by the Clinic for Neurology, Stereotaxic and Psychiatry with innovative methods that enable targeted influencing of brain activity by means of non-invasive brain stimulation as well as neurofeedback
  • to embed non-invasive brain stimulation methods into a coherent overall concept with psychopharmacological, psychotherapeutic and psychosocial interventions, thus ensuring individualized and integrative care
  • clinical and preclinical research completes patient care, so that the TNL forms a platform for clinical translation so that cutting edge research findings and methods are directly translated into effective treatment approaches
